Best Spices for Achar

Best Spices for Achar: The Secret to Authentic Flavor

The rich taste of achar comes from the perfect blend of aromatic spices. Here are the essential ones:
✔ Mustard Seeds (Rai) – Bold, tangy flavor & aids digestion.

✔ Fenugreek Seeds (Methi Dana) – Nutty taste & gut-friendly.

✔ Fennel Seeds (Saunf) – Sweet aroma & cooling effect.

✔ Turmeric (Haldi) – Natural preservative & vibrant color.

✔ Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch) – Adds heat & enhances taste.

✔ Asafoetida (Hing) – Strong aroma & digestion booster.

✔ Nigella Seeds (Kalonji) – Peppery taste & immunity booster.

The right spices make the perfect pickle! Try our homemade, spice-rich achars today.

DIY Achar at Home

DIY Achar at Home: Make Your Own Tasty Pickles!

Making achar at home is easy and ensures pure, preservative-free flavor. Follow these simple steps:

Ingredients:
✔ Fresh vegetables or fruits (mango, lemon, chili, garlic)
✔ Mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, fennel seeds, turmeric, red chili powder
✔ Salt, asafoetida (hing), nigella seeds (kalonji)
✔ Mustard oil or sesame oil

Method:
Wash & Dry – Clean ingredients thoroughly and dry completely.
Mix Spices – Combine all spices for a rich, aromatic.
Coat & Store – Toss ingredients with spices and mustard oil.
Sun-Dry & Ferment – Leave in sunlight for 4-7 days for enhanced flavor.
Enjoy! – Your homemade achar is ready to eat.

Make your own delicious, healthy achar and enjoy the taste of tradition!
